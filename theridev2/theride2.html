The Ride

Catfish and the Bottlemen

May 27, 2016

Catfish and the Bottlemen, the 10 year old indie band from Wales, release their second studio album after a year of touring. It reached number one on the UK Albums chart and 28 on the US Billboard 200 in 2016.
